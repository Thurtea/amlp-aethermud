# Chi-Town Domain
**Location:** Coalition States Illinois Territory  
**Type:** Fortress City  
**Population:** 2 million+ (humans only)

## Overview
Chi-Town is the capital and largest fortress city of the Coalition States. It represents the pinnacle of human military might in Rifts Earth, featuring towering walls, advanced technology, and strict anti-magic laws.

## Key Features:
- **The Burbs:** Sprawling slums outside the walls (40+ million squatters)
- **Inner City:** Gleaming metropolis for CS citizens only
- **Military Complex:** SAMAS squadrons, Skull imagery everywhere
- **Emperor Prosek:** Rules with iron fist from central fortress
- **Anti-Magic Policy:** Magic users executed on sight
- **Dog Boys:** Mutant soldiers patrol everywhere
