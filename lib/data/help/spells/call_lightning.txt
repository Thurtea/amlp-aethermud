CALL_LIGHTNING (Spell - Warlock/Mystic)
===================================
PPE Cost: 9
Damage: 6d6 MD (area)
Range: Sky/Area
Area: 15-foot radius (strikes from above)

Calls down a stormbolt from the sky to strike multiple targets
in the area, dealing heavy electrical damage and possibly
stunning or knocking targets off balance.

See also: help magic, help weather
