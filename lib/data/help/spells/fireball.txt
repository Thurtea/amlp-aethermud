FIREBALL (Spell - Warlock/Mystic)
===================================
PPE Cost: 5
Damage: 4d6 MD
Range: 60 feet
Area: 10-foot radius

Launches a concentrated ball of fire that explodes on impact,
dealing mega-damage to all targets within the blast radius.
All NPCs and players in the room are hit simultaneously.

See also: help magic, help psionics
