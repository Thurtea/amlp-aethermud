GLOBE_OF_DAYLIGHT (Spell - Warlock/Mystic)
===================================
PPE Cost: 7
Damage: 0 MD (Utility)
Range: Self
Area: 20-foot radius

Creates a brilliant globe of daylight that illuminates the area,
negates magical darkness, and damages or blinds undead and shadow
creatures for the duration.

See also: help magic, help light
