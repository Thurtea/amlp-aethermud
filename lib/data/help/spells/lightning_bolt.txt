LIGHTNING_BOLT (Spell - Warlock/Mystic)
===================================
PPE Cost: 6
Damage: 5d6 MD
Range: 80 feet
Area: Single-target line

Fires a concentrated bolt of electrical energy that arcs toward
a target, potentially jumping to nearby foes. High damage to a
single target with chance to stun briefly.

See also: help magic, help psionics
