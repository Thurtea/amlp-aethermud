TELEPORT (Spell - Warlock/Mystic)
===================================
PPE Cost: 10
Damage: 0 MD (Utility)
Range: Self/Touch (targeted teleport)
Area: Single target

Instantly transports the caster or a willing target to a known
location or to another player. Highly disruptive if used in
combat; some areas may block teleportation.

See also: help magic, help rift
