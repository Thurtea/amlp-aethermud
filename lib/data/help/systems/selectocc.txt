selectocc - Choose your Occupational Character Class (O.C.C.) after chargen

Usage:
  selectocc <occ_id>

Description:
  After character creation you may choose to select an O.C.C. (Occupational
  Character Class) at any time using this command. This will apply the OCC's
  primary skills, abilities, and any starting spells or powers to your
  character. Use 'help occs' or 'help systems/occs' to list and inspect
  available OCCs.

Examples:
  selectocc rogue_scientist

Notes:
  - You must meet an OCC's stat and race requirements to select it.
  - This action will be saved to your character file.
