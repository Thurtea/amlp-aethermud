# New Camelot Domain
**Location:** England (Rifts Earth)  
**Type:** Medieval Kingdom  
**Ruler:** King Arthur (returned)

## Overview
New Camelot is Arthur Pendragon's reborn kingdom in Rifts England. It blends Arthurian legend with high technology, featuring knights in power armor and mystic advisors. The kingdom represents hope and justice in a chaotic world.

## Key Features:
- **Camelot Castle:** Arthur's seat of power
- **Knights of the Round Table:** Elite warriors (power armor + magic)
- **Merlin's Tower:** Mystic studies and research
- **Market Square:** Trading hub for Europe
- **Training Grounds:** Knight academy
- **Liberal Policies:** Magic/D-Bees welcome (unlike CS)
