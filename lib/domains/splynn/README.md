# Splynn Domain
**Location:** Atlantis (Dimensional Marketplace)  
**Ruler:** Splugorth Splynncryth  
**Type:** Slave Market & Dimensional Bazaar

## Overview
Splynn is the most infamous dimensional marketplace in the megaverse. Run by the alien Splugorth intelligence named Splynncryth, it's a city where ANYTHING can be bought or sold—including sentient beings. It's simultaneously wondrous and horrifying.

## Key Features:
- **Dimensional Markets:** Buy/sell across dimensions
- **Slave Markets:** Largest in the megaverse (horrifying)
- **Bio-Wizardry:** Unique magic-technology fusion
- **Dimensional Gates:** Portals to other worlds
- **Splugorth Law:** Strict but fair (within marketplace)
- **No Escape:** Slave collars, bio-wizard controls
- **Extreme Wealth:** Credits, gems, artifacts, lives
