BASIC COMMANDS

  help                Show help topics or help <topic>
  look / l            Look around the room
  inventory / i       Check carried items
  stats / score       View your character sheet
  skills              Show all known skills
  pskills / sskills   Primary / secondary skills
  save                Save your character
  quit / logout       Save and disconnect
  examine <target>    Inspect an object or player
  brief               Toggle brief room descriptions
