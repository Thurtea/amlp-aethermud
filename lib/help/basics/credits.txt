CREDITS

Credits are the universal currency on Rifts Earth. Used for
trading, rift travel, and purchasing services.

COMMANDS
  credits / balance   Check your current credit balance

STARTING CREDITS
  Most characters start with 1,000 credits. Some OCCs vary:
    Dragon Hatchling    500 credits
    Sunaj Assassin      800 credits
    All others          1,000 credits

SPENDING CREDITS
  Rift travel           500 credits per jump
  (More uses coming in future updates)

See also: help rift, help equipment
