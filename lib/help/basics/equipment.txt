EQUIPMENT AND INVENTORY

  inventory / i       List items you are carrying
  equip <item>        Equip an item (weapon, armor, accessory)
  unequip <item>      Remove an equipped item
  worn                Show currently equipped items
  get <item>          Pick up an item from the room
  drop <item>         Drop an item on the ground
  examine <item>      Inspect an item's details

Equipment slots: primary weapon, secondary weapon, armor,
and up to 3 accessories.
