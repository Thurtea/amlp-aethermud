EXPERIENCE AND LEVELS

Experience points (XP) are earned through combat, quests, and
exploration. As you gain levels, your abilities improve.

XP SOURCES
  Combat kills          Variable based on target difficulty
  Quest completion      Awarded by game content
  Wizard awards         Admins can grant XP via 'award' command

LEVEL PROGRESSION
  Level 1    0 XP          Level 6    16,000 XP
  Level 2    2,000 XP      Level 7    24,000 XP
  Level 3    4,000 XP      Level 8    34,000 XP
  Level 4    8,000 XP      Level 9    50,000 XP
  Level 5    12,000 XP     Level 10   70,000 XP

PER-LEVEL GAINS
  +1d6 HP
  +1 attack per round (every 3 levels)
  Skill percentages increase

DEATH
  You have 5 lives. Dying costs 1 life. At 0 lives, the character
  is permanently dead. Deaths may leave scars.
