CHARACTER STATS

AetherMUD uses the Palladium RPG stat system with 8 attributes:

  IQ  - Intelligence Quotient   Affects: skill learning, tech use
  ME  - Mental Endurance         Affects: psionic resistance, willpower
  MA  - Mental Affinity          Affects: persuasion, trust, leadership
  PS  - Physical Strength        Affects: melee damage, carry capacity
  PP  - Physical Prowess         Affects: strike/parry bonuses
  PE  - Physical Endurance       Affects: max HP, poison/disease saves
  PB  - Physical Beauty          Affects: social interactions
  SPD - Speed                    Affects: movement, initiative

Stats are rolled as 3d6 (range 3-18) during character creation.
Exceptional stats (16+) grant additional bonuses.

DERIVED VALUES
  HP   = PE + 1d6 per level    Hit Points
  SDC  = 20 base               Structural Damage Capacity
  MDC  = Race-specific         Mega Damage Capacity (dragons, etc.)
  ISP  = OCC-based             Inner Strength Points (psionics)
  PPE  = OCC-based             Potential Psychic Energy (magic)
