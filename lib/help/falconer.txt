FALCONER - Aldric's Falconry Shop (New Camelot)
================================================

Aldric's Falconry is a barter shop in New Camelot, accessible from
the stables. Aldric does not accept credits -- he trades rare birds
for rare goods.

LOCATION:
  New Camelot -> Stables -> Falconer (out from stables)

TRADE TABLE:
  10 x E-Clips (energy ammo)   ->  Hawk Egg       [Level 3 Hawk]
  TX-500 Laser Rifle            ->  Master Hawk Egg [Level 5 Master Hawk]
  Coalition States Uniform      ->  Falcon Egg      [Level 7 Falcon]

HOW TO TRADE:
  1. Obtain the required items (from combat loot, shops, etc.)
  2. Go to Aldric's Falconry in New Camelot
  3. Type: trade <item>
     Examples:
       trade e-clips
       trade rifle
       trade uniform

  4. You receive a hawk egg
  5. Leave the shop and type: hatch egg
  6. Your hawk hatches and bonds with you

NOTES:
  - You must have ALL required items (10 clips for basic hawk)
  - The TX-500 and Coalition Uniform are consumed on trade
  - Falconry skill improves hatch success and hawk obedience
  - Multiple hawks can be owned but only one bonds as primary

See also: help falconry, help pets, help skills
