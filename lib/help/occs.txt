O.C.C.s (OCCUPATIONAL CHARACTER CLASSES)

IMPORTANT: Players do NOT select an OCC during character generation.
Your character starts with O.C.C.: Pending after chargen completes.

TO REQUEST AN OCC:
  tell <wizard> I would like the O.C.C. <name>
  Example: tell Thurtea I would like the O.C.C. Cyber-Knight

Wizards assign OCCs via command. Once assigned, you receive:
  - Primary skills (5 skills from your OCC package)
  - OCC-specific abilities, psionics, or magic
  - Stat bonuses where applicable

RCC RACES (Race = Class):
  Dragon races automatically receive "Dragon Hatchling RCC" during
  chargen. RCC characters may optionally request an OCC as well.
