CYBER-KNIGHT (O.C.C.)

Description:
  Cyber-Knights are techno-warriors who blend cybernetic
  enhancement with psionic discipline. They are champions of
  justice, wandering Rifts Earth to protect the innocent.
  The order follows a strict code of honor.

Abilities:
  - Psi-Sword: Manifest a psychic blade at will
  - Cyber Armor: Psionic force field (30 MDC)
  - ISP Pool: 40 starting ISP
  - SDC Bonus: +50 SDC

Starting Skills:
  Primary (auto-assigned):
    Weapon Proficiency (Sword), Body Building,
    Hand-to-Hand Expert, Lore: Demons & Monsters,
    Detect Ambush

Starting Equipment:
  Vibro-Blade, Light EBA, Food Ration, Water Canteen

Starting Credits: 1,000

Role-play Notes:
  Cyber-Knights follow a code of chivalry. They oppose
  tyranny (especially the Coalition States) and protect
  the weak. They are widely respected across Rifts Earth.
