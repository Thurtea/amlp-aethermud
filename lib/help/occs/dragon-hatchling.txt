DRAGON HATCHLING (R.C.C.)

Description:
  Dragon Hatchlings are young dragons, among the most powerful
  beings on Rifts Earth. As an RCC (Racial Character Class),
  the dragon's race IS their class -- they do not select a
  separate OCC.

Abilities:
  - MDC Creature: 200 base MDC (no HP/SDC)
  - Natural Weapons: Claws, bite, tail
  - Flight: Natural flight ability
  - Psionics: Innate psionic powers
  - Magic: Innate magical ability
  - Fire Breath: Ranged natural attack

Starting Skills:
  All skills are secondary (no OCC primary skills).
  Choose 3 during character creation.

Starting Equipment:
  None (dragons rely on natural abilities)

Starting Credits: 500

Available Dragon Races:
  Great Horned Dragon, Fire Dragon, Ice Dragon,
  Thunder Lizard, Serpent of the Wind, and more.

Role-play Notes:
  Dragons are feared and respected. Most humanoids will
  react with caution or hostility. Young dragons often
  disguise themselves to interact with smaller races.

See also: help occs, help psionics, help magic
