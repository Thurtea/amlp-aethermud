LEY LINE WALKER (O.C.C.)

Description:
  Ley Line Walkers are masters of magical ley line energy.
  They can sense, tap, and manipulate the mystical rivers
  of power that crisscross Rifts Earth. Among the most
  powerful magic users in the world.

Abilities:
  - PPE Pool: Variable (OCC-based)
  - Spell Casting: Wide range of spells
  - Ley Line Sense: Detect ley lines and nexus points
  - Ley Line Phasing: Walk along ley lines at speed

Starting Skills:
  Primary (auto-assigned):
    Lore: Magic, Language: Dragonese, Literacy,
    Mathematics, Wilderness Survival

Starting Equipment:
  TW Fire Bolt Staff, Healing Potion,
  Food Ration, Water Canteen

Starting Credits: 1,000

Role-play Notes:
  Ley Line Walkers are scholars as much as warriors.
  They seek magical knowledge and often serve as advisors
  to communities near ley lines. The Coalition considers
  them dangerous enemies.
