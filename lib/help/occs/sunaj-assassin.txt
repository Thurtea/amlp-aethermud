SUNAJ ASSASSIN (O.C.C.)

Description:
  The Sunaj are elite Atlantean assassins who serve the
  mysterious Aerihman clan. They are masters of stealth,
  shadow magic, and silent killing. Access is restricted
  to Atlantean characters who join Clan Aerihman.

Requirements:
  - Race: Must be Atlantean
  - Clan: Must join Clan Aerihman (use 'clan aerihman')
  - Note: Cannot be selected during chargen

Abilities:
  - PPE Pool: 30 starting PPE
  - ISP Pool: 20 starting ISP
  - Shadow Skills: Stealth and infiltration
  - Assassin Training: Enhanced combat techniques

Starting Skills:
  Primary (auto-assigned on clan join):
    Prowl, Detect Ambush, Intelligence,
    Weapon Proficiency (Knife), Tracking

Starting Equipment:
  Vibro-Blade (shadow weapon), Light EBA (shadow suit),
  Food Ration, Water Canteen

Starting Credits: 800

How to Unlock:
  1. Create an Atlantean character
  2. Complete chargen normally (default: Vagabond)
  3. Type: clan aerihman
  4. Your OCC changes to Sunaj Assassin

See also: help occs, help clan, help magic
