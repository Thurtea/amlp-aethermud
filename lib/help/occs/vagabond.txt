VAGABOND (O.C.C.)

Description:
  The Vagabond is the jack-of-all-trades wanderer of Rifts Earth.
  Unskilled but adaptable, they survive on wits, luck, and a
  willingness to do whatever it takes. Many adventurers begin as
  Vagabonds before finding their true calling.

Abilities:
  - No special powers or magic
  - Broad secondary skill selection
  - Adaptable to any play style

Starting Skills:
  Primary (auto-assigned):
    Wilderness Survival, First Aid, Land Navigation,
    Climbing, General Athletics

  Secondary (choose 3 during chargen):
    Selected from full secondary skill list

Starting Equipment:
  Steel Knife, Food Ration, Water Canteen

Starting Credits: 1,000

Role-play Notes:
  Vagabonds are the everyman of Rifts Earth. They might be
  farmers displaced by war, traveling merchants, or simply
  people trying to survive in a dangerous world. Wizards can
  reassign your OCC later with 'set <name> occ <class>'.

See also: help occs, help stats, help skills
