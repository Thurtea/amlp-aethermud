SIRNAME - Honorific Title System

Sirnames are honorific titles displayed before your character name.
Example: Lord Splynncryth, Lady Arismal, Baron Thurtea

REQUIREMENTS:
  - Level 10 or higher

HOW TO REQUEST:
  Players do not set their own sirname. Instead:
  1. Reach level 10
  2. Tell a wizard your desired sirname:
     tell <wizard> I would like the sirname Lord
  3. A wizard will grant it if approved

  Your request is also broadcast to all online staff:
     [Staff] PlayerName requests sirname: Lord

WIZARD USAGE:
  sirname <player> <title>    Grant a sirname
  Example: sirname Splynncryth Lord

DISPLAY:
  Once granted, your sirname appears before your name:
    Lord Splynncryth is standing around.
    Lord Splynncryth says: Hello!

RULES:
  - Sirnames must be 2-20 characters
  - Letters and spaces only
  - Must be appropriate and non-offensive
  - Wizards may deny inappropriate requests
