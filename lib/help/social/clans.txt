CLANS

Clans are organizations that characters can join for special
abilities and role-play opportunities.

COMMANDS
  clan                View your current clan status
  clan <name>         Join a clan (if eligible)

AVAILABLE CLANS
  Aerihman            Atlantean secret society
    Requirement: Atlantean race
    Benefit: Unlocks Sunaj Assassin OCC
    Command: clan aerihman

JOINING A CLAN
  Type 'clan aerihman' to request membership. You must meet
  the race requirement. Once joined, your OCC changes and
  new skills are assigned automatically.

See also: help sunaj-assassin, help occs
