COMMUNICATION COMMANDS

  say <msg>           Speak in your current language
  ooc <msg>           Out-of-character talk (no language)
  tell <player> <msg> Send a private message
  reply <msg>         Reply to the last tell you received
  chat <msg>          Global chat channel
  shout <msg>         Shout to nearby rooms
  whisper <who> <msg> Whisper to someone in the room
  emote <action>      Perform a custom emote
  converse            Toggle conversation mode (. to exit)
  speak <language>    Change your speaking language
  languages / langs   List your known languages
