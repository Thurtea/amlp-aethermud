SOCIAL COMMANDS

  introduce <player>  Introduce yourself to another player
  remember            List players you have been introduced to
  who                 Show players currently online
  title <text>        Set your title on the who list
  clan                View your clan status
  clan aerihman       Join the Aerihman clan (Atlantean only)
  credits / balance   Check your credit balance

Until you introduce yourself, other players see you by race
(e.g. "A human") rather than by name.
