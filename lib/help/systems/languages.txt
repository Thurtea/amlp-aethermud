LANGUAGES

Characters know one or more languages based on their race.
The language system affects the 'say' command.

COMMANDS
  speak <language>    Switch your speaking language
  languages / langs   List your known languages

LANGUAGE EFFECTS
  When you use 'say', your message is spoken in your current
  language. Listeners who know the language see your words.
  Listeners who don't see: "says something unintelligible."

  Use 'ooc' to speak out-of-character (bypasses language).

STARTING LANGUAGES (by race)
  Human/Default       American
  Elf                 Elven, American
  Dwarf               Dwarven, American
  Dragon              Dragonese, American
  Atlantean           American, Euro
  Goblin/Orc/Troll    Gobblely, American
  Demon/Deevil        Demonic, American
  Faerie/Pixie        Faerie, American
