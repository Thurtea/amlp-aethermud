MAGIC AND PSIONICS

  cast <spell>        Cast a magic spell (costs PPE)
  spells              List your known spells
  ppe                 Show current PPE pool
  meditate            Regenerate PPE faster

  use <power>         Use a psionic power (costs ISP)
  powers              List your known psionic powers
  isp                 Show current ISP pool

Spells and powers are assigned based on your O.C.C.
PPE regenerates slowly over time; meditate speeds it up.
ISP regenerates passively.
