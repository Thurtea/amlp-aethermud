PSIONICS

Psionics are mental powers fueled by Inner Strength Points (ISP).
Many OCCs have innate psionic abilities.

COMMANDS
  use <power>         Activate a psionic power
  powers              List your known powers
  isp                 Show current ISP pool

ISP POOL
  ISP regenerates passively over time. The rate depends on
  your OCC and Mental Endurance (ME) stat.

PSIONIC CATEGORIES
  Healing       Psychic surgery, bio-regeneration
  Physical      Telekinesis, ectoplasm
  Sensitive     Telepathy, clairvoyance, sixth sense
  Super         Mind bolt, pyrokinesis, hydrokinesis

PSIONIC OCCS
  Mind Melter       Master of all psionic disciplines
  Burster           Pyrokinetic specialist
  Psi-Healer        Healing psionic
  Psi-Stalker       Anti-magic psionic hunter
  Cyber-Knight      Psi-sword and cyber armor
