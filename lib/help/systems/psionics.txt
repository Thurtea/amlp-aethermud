PSIONICS

Psionics are mental powers fueled by Inner Strength Points (ISP).
Many OCCs have innate psionic abilities.

COMMANDS
  use <power>         Activate a psionic power
  powers              List your known powers
  isp                 Show current ISP pool

NOTE: Psionic abilities are assigned when your OCC is set. If you
have no powers listed, request an OCC from a wizard:
  tell <wizard> I would like the O.C.C. <name>

ISP POOL
  ISP regenerates passively over time. The rate depends on
  your OCC and Mental Endurance (ME) stat.

  Starting ISP varies by OCC:
    Mind Melter       60 ISP
    Burster           50 ISP
    Cyber-Knight      40 ISP
    Psi-Healer        45 ISP
    Psi-Stalker       35 ISP

PSIONIC CATEGORIES
  Healing       Psychic surgery, bio-regeneration, healing touch
  Physical      Telekinesis, ectoplasm, levitation
  Sensitive     Telepathy, clairvoyance, sixth sense, empathy
  Super         Mind bolt, pyrokinesis, hydrokinesis, electrokinesis

PSIONIC OCCS
  Mind Melter       Master of all psionic disciplines
  Burster           Pyrokinetic specialist
  Psi-Healer        Healing psionic
  Psi-Stalker       Anti-magic psionic hunter
  Cyber-Knight      Psi-sword and cyber armor

NON-PSIONIC OCCS
  Some OCCs have minor psionic abilities (1-3 powers).
  Dragon Hatchlings have innate psionic powers from their race.
