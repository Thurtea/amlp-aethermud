SKILLS

Skills represent your character's learned abilities. Each skill
has a percentage chance of success that improves with use.

COMMANDS
  skills              Show all known skills
  pskills             Show primary skills (from OCC)
  sskills             Show secondary skills (player-chosen)

SKILL TYPES
  Primary Skills    Auto-assigned from your OCC (5 skills)
  Secondary Skills  Chosen during chargen (3 skills)

SKILL IMPROVEMENT
  Skills improve through use. Each successful use adds to the
  skill's experience counter. At certain thresholds, the
  percentage increases.

SKILL CATEGORIES
  Combat              Weapon proficiencies, martial arts
  Technical           Mechanics, electronics, demolitions
  Medical             First aid, paramedic, surgery
  Wilderness          Survival, tracking, land navigation
  Language            Languages and literacy
  Lore                Knowledge of magic, demons, history
  Physical            Athletics, climbing, swimming
  Rogue               Prowl, pick locks, streetwise
