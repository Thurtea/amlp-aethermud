WIZARD CASTLE
==============

The Wizard Castle is the headquarters for all AetherMUD staff.

GETTING THERE:
  From Staff Castle Courtyard: go 'up' to Wizard Courtyard
  From Wizard Courtyard: go 'north' to Main Hall
  From your workroom: type 'castle'

FACILITIES:
  Courtyard     - Entrance with notice board directory
  Main Hall     - Central meeting area with fireplace
  Dog Spike     - Castle mascot's corner (pet/feed)
  Training      - Combat testing grounds with training dummy
  Monitor       - Mortal observation station (scan/watch)
  Elevator      - Access to wizard role offices

ROLE OFFICES (via elevator):
  Domain Office   - World building workspace and references
  Coding Office   - Systems and debugging workspace
  Roleplay Office - Events and storytelling workspace
  Admin Office    - Administration (Admin only, restricted)

ELEVATOR USAGE:
  In the elevator room, type:
    press domain     - Go to Domain office
    press coding     - Go to Coding office
    press roleplay   - Go to Roleplay office
    press admin      - Go to Admin office (Admin only)

NOTICE BOARD:
  In the courtyard, 'examine board' or 'read board' for the
  full facility directory.

PERSONAL WORKROOMS:
  Type 'home' from anywhere in the castle to return to your
  personal workroom. Your workroom has a 'castle' exit back.
