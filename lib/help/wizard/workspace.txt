WIZARD WORKSPACE
=================

Each wizard gets a personal workspace for building game content.

LOCATION:
  /domains/wizard/YOURNAME/

STRUCTURE:
  /domains/wizard/YOURNAME/
    workroom.lpc          Your personal workroom
    area/                 QCS workspace
      npc/                NPC templates
      room/               Room templates
      weapon/             Weapon templates
      armor/              Armor templates
      item/               Item templates

ACCESSING YOUR WORKSPACE:
  - Your workroom: type 'home' from wizard castle
  - Your workspace files are managed via QCS commands

SECURITY:
  - You can only modify files in YOUR workspace
  - Admins can access and modify any wizard's workspace
  - Roleplay wizards do not have QCS building access

CREATING CONTENT:
  1. create npc goblin           Creates area/npc/goblin.lpc
  2. modify goblin short "..."   Edits the template
  3. deploy goblin here          Clones into current room

WORKSPACE SETUP:
  Workspace directories are automatically created when you are
  promoted to wizard. If directories are missing, create content
  with QCS and they will be auto-created.

See also: help qcs, help building, help wizard_roles
